## Introduction

FTMS is a modern web application built to help tailoring businesses manage their workflow. It handles customers, orders, measurements, inventory, and employee tasks in a unified interface.

## Getting Started

To access the system, navigate to the login page.

- **Login**: Enter your username and password.
- **Roles**: The system supports different roles (e.g., Admin, Tailor). Admins have full access, while other roles may have restricted permissions.

## Dashboard

Upon logging in, you are greeted by the Dashboard.

- **Key Metrics**: View total orders, revenue, active jobs, and low stock alerts at a glance.
- **Charts**: Visual representation of monthly sales and order status distribution.
- **Recent Activity**: Quick view of the latest orders and status changes.

## Order Management

The core of the system.

- **View Orders**: A searchable, sortable table of all orders.
- **Create Order**: Link a customer, select a style, set a delivery date, and record the price.
- **Track Status**: Update order status through the workflow: `Pending` → `In Progress` → `Ready` → `Delivered`.
- **Invoicing**: Generate and print professional invoices for any order.

## Customer Management

Maintain a detailed database of your clients.

- **Customer Profiles**: Store contact details (Phone, Email, Address).
- **Measurement History**: Access previous measurements for repeat orders.
- **Communication**: Quick links to WhatsApp for easy communication.

## Inventory & Materials

Track your fabric and sewing supplies.

- **Material List**: View current stock levels, unit costs, and suppliers.
- **Low Stock Alerts**: The system highlights items that are running low to prevent shortages.
- **Management**: Add new materials or update quantities as stock is used or replenished.

## Job Cards & Production

Manage the production floor and employee tasks.

- **Assign Jobs**: Create job cards linked to specific orders and assign them to employees.
- **Deadlines**: Set and track deadlines for each job.
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor jobs as they move from `Pending` to `Completed`.

## Measurements & Templates

Flexible measurement system.

- **Templates (Item Types)**: Define measurement templates for different clothing items (e.g., "Shirt", "Trousers", "Suit"). Define fields like "Collar", "Chest", "Sleeve".
- **Recording**: Record specific measurements for customers based on these templates.

## Styles Catalog

A visual gallery of available designs.

- **Gallery**: Browse style images and descriptions.
- **Management**: Add new styles with images to show customers options.

## Reports

Generate insights into business performance.

- **Order Reports**: Filter orders by date range to see sales volume and revenue.
- **Customer Reports**: Export customer lists.
- **Employee Reports**: View employee status and roles.
- **Printing**: All reports are printer-friendly.

## User Management

(Admin Only)

- **Manage Staff**: Add new user accounts for employees.
- **Roles**: Assign roles (Admin, User) to control access levels.
- **Status**: Activate or deactivate user accounts.
